Our Story & Values

Amoy Bayan was born from a deep love for Filipino culture and a desire to share its unique essence with the world. We believe that every scent tells a story, evokes a memory, and connects us to our roots. Our journey began with a simple idea: to capture the nostalgic and heartwarming aromas of the Philippines in beautifully handcrafted candles.

Our Mission

To share the warmth, scent, and soul of the Filipino culture through handcrafted candles made with locally-inspired fragrances.

Our Vision

To become the leading Filipino candle brand that lights homes and hearts with the distinct and nostalgic aromas of the Philippinesâ€”celebrating culture, memory, and identity.

Why Choose Amoy Bayan?

  • Authentically Filipino: Our scents are inspired by the diverse landscapes, traditions, and flavors of the Philippines.
  • Handcrafted with Care: Each candle is lovingly poured by hand, ensuring quality and attention to detail.
  • Locally Sourced (where possible): We strive to use locally sourced materials and support Filipino artisans.
  • Eco-Conscious: We are committed to using natural waxes and sustainable practices.
